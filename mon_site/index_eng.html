Who are we ?

Since Nest joined the Google Hardware team last year, we've been working to simplify the connected home to make it more useful in everyday life. We want to invent a home where the products work together seamlessly, yet are easy to set up and manage. That's why we decided to bring Nest and Google Home products together under one roof. Get to know Google Nest. From Google Assistant speakers to thermostats, our products work together to keep you informed, comfortable and safe, to watch over your home while you're away, and to keep you connected to your loved ones. Welcome to the home designed to help you, by Google.
